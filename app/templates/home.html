How To Guide

Adding a Show to Your List

Click add show button. Type in title of your show. Click submit. Your show will appear at the top of the page.

Removing a Show from Your List

Click remove show button on the top right of the show you want to delete

Searching for a Show

Locate search bar at top of page. Click on the white box. Type in the title of the show. Click search. The show will appear. To get rid of your results click the clear button
